About Me

Who Am I

Hi, I'm Thomas — a Multi-Media Production student based in Zurich, Switzerland.

I've lived in Cambridge, Madrid, and Berlin, and traveled to 72 countries. Those experiences shaped how I think about design, communication, and what needs people around the globe might have.

Today, I work as a freelance web developer and digital marketer with 4+ years of experience, helping small businesses build modern, user-friendly websites, make an appealing social media , branding and Advertizing. Basically I am a secretary for everything online.
